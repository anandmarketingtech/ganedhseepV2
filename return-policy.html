Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase from Ganeshdeep Knitwear. If you are not satisfied for any reason, we are here to help.

Returns & Exchanges

  • Valid within 7 days of the delivery date.
  • Items must be in their original condition: unused, unwashed, and with all tags attached in the original packaging.
  • Please note that sale or custom-made items are final and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Size or Color Issue

  • We are happy to accept exchanges for size or color mismatches, subject to availability.
  • To initiate an exchange, please email us with your Order ID and the details of the exchange. We'll guide you through the process.

Return Process

  • To start a return, email us at: support@ganeshdeepknitwear.com.
  • Once your return is approved, pack the item securely and ship it to the return address we provide.
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to an error on our part.

Refunds

  • Once we receive and inspect your item, we will notify you of the approval of your refund.
  • Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 working days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original method of payment or as store credit.
